**Prevention Checklist**



Stay Safe Online – Simple Checklist



\- Never share your OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank

\- Always double-check the sender before trusting any message or email

\- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links, no matter how urgent they seem

\- Use strong and different passwords for your accounts

\- Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security

\- Always check the UPI ID and name before sending money

\- Avoid making payments using public Wi-Fi

\- Download apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store

\- If something feels suspicious, report it on cybercrime.gov.in

\- In case of fraud, immediately call helpline number 1930
